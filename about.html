What is off grid living?

The simple answer: off grid means living disconnected from the public power grid. But there’s a lot more to it than that. We believe off grid living represents a lifestyle shift toward a more self-sufficient existence, whether you live in a cabin in the woods or an inner-city apartment. We’re here to show you how.
